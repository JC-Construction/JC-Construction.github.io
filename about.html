PJ. L. Construction was started in 2006 by its owner Jonathan Judson. Jonathan has been involved in the building and construction industry since his teenage years and has a passion for building. He has experience in all phases of construction from the buying and retail level to the actual framing and complete House construction. He has worked in the Coastal area building homes in the early 1990's - he then went on to work at the retail level with Home Depot and Robert Bowden, Inc. from 1996 - 2003. While doing this, he worked towards his degree in Construction Management at Southern Polytechnic State University. After moving back to Augusta in 2003, he went on to run the Sign Production Services for AAA Sign Company before finally deciding to get back into Building Custom Homes in 2006. Jonathan was joined by his wife, Lynn Judson, in 2009. Lynn, who holds a degree in Business, now handles all accounting activities for the company and also assists Owners in design and allowance purchases for their project. So, we are truly a family-owned and operated company AND we're all from the Augusta area.
