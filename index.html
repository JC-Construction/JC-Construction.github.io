Design-Build

subtitle

J. L. Judson Construction is happy to help you from the beginning of your project by working together with you and custom plan draftsman and engineers. This can often be beneficial to the owner as we can show you ways to save money or build more efficiently from the start. By doing this, you'll be much more likely to save in the long run by avoiding costly change-orders or overages on allowances. If you are just considering building or remodeling, give us a call and we'll work hands-on with you from the beginning.

So what's this all about?

J. L. Construction was started in 2006 by its owner Jonathan Judson. Jonathan has been involved in the building and construction industry since his teenage years and has a passion for building. He has experience in all phases of construction from the buying and retail level to the actual framing and complete House construction.

He has worked in the Coastal area building homes in the early 1990's - he then went on to work at the retail level with Home Depot and Robert Bowden, Inc. from 1996 - 2003. While doing this, he worked towards his degree in Construction Management at Southern Polytechnic State University.
